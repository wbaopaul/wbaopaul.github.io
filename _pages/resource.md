The Yu Lab develops open-source tools for bulk and single-cell omics analysis.

- [scATAC-pro](https://github.com/wbaopaul/scATAC-pro)
A comprehensive pipeline for single-cell ATAC-seq data analysis

- [GMAP](https://github.com/wbaopaul/rGMAP)
Identify topologically associated domains and subdomains in Hi-C data

- [LiMACC](https://github.com/wbaopaul/LiMACC)
Identify chromatin interations from promoter Capture Hi-C data.
