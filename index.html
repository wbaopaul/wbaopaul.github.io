Welcome to the Yu Lab

Our lab operates at the intersection of genomics, data science and cancer biology. Opening in July 2026, we aim to uncover intra- and inter-cellular mechanisms of cancer cell plasticity, the ability of cancer cells to transit between distinct phenotypic states to resist treatment and evade immune surveillance. We will leverage cutting-edge single-cell and spatial omics** technologies, statistical and machine learning approaches, along with collaborative functional studies. Our research focuses on several key areas:

  • Transcriptional and epigenetic regulation
  • Cell-cell communication within the tumor microenvironmentm
  • The impact of inflammation on the plasticity of cancer cells
